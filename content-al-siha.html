Every Project Starts with A Story

Al-Siha wanted to ensure value proposition to given to customers while they look for mango fruits. Plus, the client wanted to share valuable information and the story about how they started their journey to bring Al-Siha into reality. Markosis came up with a 4 Step Strategy.

S.P.V.B Model

While Al-Siha put their idea on the table, Markosis was strategizing to implement them to perfection. The only idea coming around for content marketing was S.P.V.B Model- Strategic planning, Positioning, Value and Business Goals. With these 4 elements onboard, Markosis perfected content marketing to the website for growth.

#1 Strategic Planning

As the first stage of content marketing, Markosis wanted to find out the target audience first, who they are going to target. Then the ideation process was followed by ways to deliver the content, and finding out KPIs for the content to find out how much they were giving in the targeted results.

#2 Positioning the brand

Al-Siha was distributing chemical free pure and fresh mango all over the country. Once the ideation was done, Markosis solely focused on the brand persona, archetype, unique selling proposition and irresistible offers for promotion.

#3 Value

As the brand planning and idea generation was done, Markosis planned to share value via the content. That means, the team first found out what value they should input on the content, that was completely based on what potential customers look for. And the content structure was planned to be different than other competitors to stand out easily.

#4 Business Goals

After the third stage, Markosis setup the KPI, business goals and the targeted results. Based on that, Markosis started writing contents that educate, entertain and engage visitors about Mangoes, which mangoes taste better, when they should eat mangoes, health benefits of mangoes and ways to preserve them. That way, the visitors were delighted with the crispy information Al-Siha shared on their website and the conversion rate started going off the roof.
